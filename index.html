ADN Nursing Simulation: Alcohol Withdrawal

Scenario: "The Tremors in Room 304"

Learning Objectives

  • Identify physiological signs of alcohol withdrawal.
  • Accurately administer the CIWA-Ar scale.
  • Implement safety measures and pharmacologic interventions.

Pre-Brief: You are the primary nurse for Robert Miller. He was admitted yesterday for cellulitis. Review his chart before entering the room.
